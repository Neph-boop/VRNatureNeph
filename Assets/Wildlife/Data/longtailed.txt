LongDescription: Long-tailed shrike, locally called tarat or mamumugot, is a beautiful resident bird with a  black head, gray mantle, orange-y lower parts, and a long black tail. Like its migrant relative, the brown shrike, it is aggressive and predatory. It perches out in the open scanning for insects and small animals like lizards.

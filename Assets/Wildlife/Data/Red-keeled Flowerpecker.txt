speciesName: Red-keeled Flowerpecker

hintDescription: Look up and spot the small bird with a red stripe down the middle of their breast
LongDescription: Red-keeled Flowerpecker, locally known as tiptip, tamsi, or pipit,is endemic to the Philippines. It is one of the smallest birds you might spot on campus. They have a blue-black back and white underpants accentuated by a bright red stripe down the middle of their breast.
